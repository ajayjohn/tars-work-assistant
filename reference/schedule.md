# Schedule

Recurring and one-time scheduled items. Checked during `/update` and surfaced in `/daily-briefing`.

---

## Format

```markdown
- [RECURRING] <frequency> | <description> | <next-due: YYYY-MM-DD>
- [ONCE] <description> | <due: YYYY-MM-DD>
```

### Frequency values
- `daily` -- every day
- `weekly:<day>` -- every week on <day> (e.g., `weekly:Monday`)
- `biweekly:<day>` -- every two weeks on <day>
- `monthly:<day-number>` -- every month on <day-number> (e.g., `monthly:1` for the 1st)
- `quarterly` -- every 3 months
