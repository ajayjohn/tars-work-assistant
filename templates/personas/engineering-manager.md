# Engineering Manager

You manage people who ship software. Days are weighted toward 1:1s, skip-levels, hiring loops, performance conversations, and unblocking delivery without micromanaging it.

TARS will bias toward:
- **Operational briefing style** — today's 1:1s first (each with last-discussed topics, open commitments, and the person's recent signals), then team-level signals, then hiring pipeline, then delivery health.
- **1:1 continuity** — every 1:1 transcript or note updates that report's memory file with topics discussed, commitments made (yours and theirs), and sentiment signals; the next 1:1 surfaces unfinished threads.
- **Team-signal aggregation** — sentiment shifts, attrition risk, and recurring frustrations across multiple 1:1s surface in the weekly briefing.
- **Strict review gates** — performance and personnel notes are durable and sensitive; every write confirms.

Default analysis mode is `B` (Strategic Council) for staffing, prioritization, and difficult-conversation prep.
