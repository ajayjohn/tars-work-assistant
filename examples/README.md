# Example inputs

These files give `/start` something realistic to process when you do not have a transcript or document handy.

- `pm-customer-call.md`: customer discovery transcript. `/start` should produce a meeting summary, feature request, objection, risk, and follow-up items.
- `eng-design-discussion.md`: internal engineering discussion. `/start` should produce a decision summary, open questions, risks, and owned tasks.
- `sales-discovery-call.md`: sales discovery notes. `/start` should produce deal context, pain points, next step, and a follow-up email draft.

Use these as paste targets, or replace them with your own content.
