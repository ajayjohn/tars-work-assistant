# KPI Definitions

KPIs are defined per initiative and used by the initiative performance reporting workflow.

## How to define KPIs

Each initiative can reference KPIs by linking to entries here. KPI definitions include:

- **Name**: Human-readable label
- **Metric**: What is measured
- **Target**: Success threshold
- **Source**: Where the data comes from
- **Frequency**: How often to measure
- **Owner**: Who is accountable
